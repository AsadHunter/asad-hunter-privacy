Privacy Policy for Asad Hunter

Asad Hunter is a Chrome extension designed to analyze publicly available advertising data from the Meta (Facebook) Ad Library.

Information Collection

Asad Hunter does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal user data. The extension only processes publicly visible ad transparency information when a user visits facebook.com/ads/library.

Permissions

The extension uses browser permissions only to operate on the Meta Ad Library and to provide ad analysis features explicitly requested by the user.

Third-Party Sharing

No personal data is shared with third parties.

User Control

All actions are user-initiated. The extension does not run background tracking or collect browsing history outside the supported website.
